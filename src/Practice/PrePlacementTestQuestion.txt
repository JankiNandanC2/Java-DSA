## üìù Problem 2: TimeShift Tunnel

### Problem Description
You are managing a **queue system** for visitors entering a tunnel. Visitors can be of three types:
- **Gold** (highest priority)
- **Silver** (medium priority)
- **Regular** (lowest priority)

### Operations
1. `ARRIVE id type`
   - Add a visitor with ID and type (`gold`, `silver`, `regular`) to the queue.
2. `PROCESS`
   - Serve the next visitor based on priority rules:
     - Gold visitors are served before Silver and Regular.
     - Silver visitors are served before Regular.
     - Within the same type, serve in **arrival order**.
   - If the queue is empty and `PROCESS` is called, ignore it.

### Output Format
- Print the IDs of visitors in the order they are processed.
